Guess Who?

Gameplay

  1. Each player receives a random character displayed on their screen, hidden from the opponent.
  2. Take turns asking yes/no questions to narrow down the characters.
  3. Use the question box to type and submit questions.
  4. Example questions: "Is your character married?" "Is your character wearing a hat?"
  5. Eliminate characters based on the opponent's answers.
  6. Click on characters to view their details.
  7. Flip down cards that don't match the answers.
  8. When confident, click on the character you think is the opponent's.
  9. Click the "Guess" button to submit your guess.
  10. Correct guess wins the game;
  11. Incorrect guess continues the game.
